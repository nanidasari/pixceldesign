Logo Design

The icon is a stylized combination of a pixel and a design tool, such as a pencil or a digital pen, forming a cohesive and innovative symbol. This represents Pixcel Design’s commitment to merging digital craftsmanship with artistic flair.

Packaging

Packaging design is a multi-faceted process of creating both the visual appearance and physical structure of a product's packaging. It combines design elements like form, materials, color, imagery, typography, and regulatory information to make a product suitable for the market. Effective packaging design protects the product, identifies it, markets the brand, attracts customers, and influences purchasing decisions

Brand Identity

A brand identity encompasses the visual, verbal, and emotional aspects that distinguish a brand. It's the combination of elements like the logo, colors, typography, tagline, and brand voice that create a unique identity for a company or product. A strong brand identity helps consumers recognize and connect with a brand, building trust and loyalty
